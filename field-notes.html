Field Notes

Field Notes is a quieter space within The Casio Archive.

While the archive documents models, modules, and technical details, this section is dedicated to something less structured: the experience of wearing, searching, finding, and living with Casio watches.

Here I write about early morning flea markets, dusty display trays, overlooked pieces, and the quiet satisfaction of recognizing a familiar module from across a table. About the patience of searching. About the moment when an ordinary object becomes a small discovery.

Personal reflections from the field. The slow process of building an archive not only through documentation, but through experience.
